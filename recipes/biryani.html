Biryani

Description

Biryani is a mixed rice dish most popular in South Asia. It is made with a special type of rice called Basmati, chicken or goat as the meat choice and spices. To cater to vegetarians, in some cases, it is prepared by substituting vegetables for the meat.

Ingredients

  • Basmati Rice
  • Chicken or Goat meat
  • Fried Onions (Birista)
  • Ghee
  • Cinnamon
  • Clove
  • Salt
  • Cardamom
  • Star Anise
  • Bay leaves
  • Shahi Jeera
  • Curd (Indian Yogurt)
  • Coriander or Cilantro Leaves

Steps

  1. Mix all the ingredients in a large pot and marinade it. Add chicken and coat well. Marinade 20 minutes to overnight.
  2. Bring water to the boil, add salt and spices.
  3. Add rice, bring back up to the boil then cook until rice is just cooked still a bit firm in the middle.
  4. Drain immediately. Set aside.
  5. Heat oil in a large pan. Fry onion, in batches until golden brown.
  6. Remove onto paper towel. Repeat with remaining onion.
  7. Place pot with chicken in it onto a stove over medium heat. Cover and cook.
  8. Remove lid. Cook turning chicken.
  9. Remove from heat.
  10. Turn chicken so skin side is down - it should cover most of the base of the pot.
  11. Scatter over half the onion then half the coriander.
  12. Top with all the rice. Gently pat down and flatten surface.
  13. Drizzle saffron across rice surface in random pattern, then drizzle over ghee.
  14. Place lid on. Return to stove over medium heat.
  15. As soon as you see steam, turn down to low then cook.
  16. Remove from stove, rest with lid for sometime.
